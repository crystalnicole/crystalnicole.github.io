WHAT WE DO

We are here to promote the culture by celebrating entities and individuals that are doing good things throughout society. We believe in the intelligence of our audience and we advocate for civic engagement, media literacy and entreprenuership.

WHO WE ARE

Crystal Carter, Editor-In-Chief

Crystal created Campaigner because she saw a lack of critical anaylsis of pop culture. She decided to create Campaigner to add promote her interest in critical thinking and for the love of her generation.

Paulette Greenhouse, Designer

Paulette is Campaigner's creative genius. Her layout and design skills are what bring this magazine to life. With a background in Journalism and graphics design Paulette is the ultimate media machine!

Lisbeth Cervantes, Fashion Designer

Bay Area transplant and now New York City fashionista, Lisbeth is snatching clothes off racks and taking names. With a background in Public Relations and pursuing a degree in Fashion Merchandise from the Fashion Institute of Technology in Manhattan, Lisbeth will bring us the latest in fashion and style!

Leo Docuyanan, Photo Director

Leo Docuyanan is probably the coolest photographer in the streets. He has shot the likes of Del the Fucky Homosapien, Yelawolf and other hip hop notables and has a huge love for muscle cars!
